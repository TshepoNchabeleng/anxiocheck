Global Theme & Visual Style

UI Style: Glassmorphism, utilizing transparent bubble icons and blurred layers for depth.

Metric: A "Blood Pressure vs. Time" Cartesian coordinate graph.

Suggestions Footer: Located at the bottom of the viewport with a transparent bubble backdrop, containing personalized text recommendations for lowering or stabilizing blood pressure through activity.
